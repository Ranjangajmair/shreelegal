About Us

Welcome to Shree Legal Practitioner’s Firm: Trusted Legal Expertise in Kathmandu, Nepal

Shree Legal Practitioner’s Firm stands as a premier full-service law firm based in Kathmandu, Nepal. Founded over four decades ago by the Senior Advocate Mr. Ramesh Prasad Dhital, Shree Legal has grown to become one of Nepal’s most trusted and respected law firm. Our foundation was built on a mission of making the legal process accessible, transparent, and highly effective for every client we serve. This mission has carried us through the years, earning us a reputation for excellence and ethical practices in every case we undertake.

As a leading law firm in Kathmandu, we provide comprehensive legal solutions to individuals, businesses, and corporations, both domestic and international. We understand that navigating legal complexities can be daunting, which is why we are committed to making the legal journey as straightforward and stress-free as possible. Whether it’s a complex business dispute, intellectual property rights, or legal advice for startups, our team of skilled and dedicated professionals brings a wealth of experience and a client-centered approach to each unique case. Shree Legal stands out as a firm that combines traditional values of trust and confidentiality with modern legal practices, allowing us to deliver innovative solutions that are grounded in integrity and respect.

Our firm’s strength lies in the collective expertise of our legal team. Each of our attorneys brings specialized knowledge and practical insight to their field, allowing us to offer a depth of service that addresses a wide range of legal needs. Over the years, our dedication to excellence has led us to assist clients from diverse backgrounds, helping them achieve favorable outcomes through reliable, insightful, and strategic legal counsel. We are committed to being more than just legal advisors—we are advocates, partners, and allies dedicated to safeguarding our clients’ rights and interests in every legal matter.

Our Mission and Values

At Shree Legal, our mission goes beyond achieving legal success. We are dedicated to creating a positive impact in the lives of our clients, contributing to the advancement of Nepal’s legal standards, and fostering an environment of growth, learning, and ethical practice. Our mission is to provide reliable, effective, and accessible legal solutions that empower our clients to make informed decisions, protect their rights, and navigate the legal system with confidence.

Our values form the foundation of our practice and guide every decision we make. These values are not just principles; they are our commitment to our clients, our team, and the broader community. They include:

  • Client-Centered Approach: We place our clients at the heart of everything we do. Understanding their unique needs and goals allows us to offer tailored solutions that are both effective and practical.

  • Integrity and Transparency: Upholding the highest standards of honesty and openness in all aspects of our work builds trust and ensures that our clients are fully informed and confident in their decisions.

  • Excellence and Dedication: We are committed to excellence in every case we handle. This means diligent research, thorough preparation, and a proactive approach to addressing challenges and finding solutions.

  • Commitment to Confidentiality: We recognize the importance of confidentiality in legal matters, and we handle all client information with the utmost discretion and respect.

These values are the pillars that shape our identity as a law firm. They reflect our dedication to achieving the best possible outcomes for our clients while maintaining the integrity and respect that are essential to our mission.
