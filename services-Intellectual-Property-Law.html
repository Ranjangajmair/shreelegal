Intellectual Property Law

In today's globalized world, protecting intellectual property (IP) is more important than ever. At Shree Legal, we offer comprehensive IP services designed to safeguard our clients' creative assets against infringement.

Trademark Registration

Trademarks are essential for establishing brand identity. Our services include:

  • Trademark Searches: We conduct thorough searches to assess the availability of proposed trademarks. This step helps clients avoid potential conflicts and make informed decisions about trademark registration.

  • Trademark Application Preparation: Our team prepares and files trademark applications with the relevant authorities, ensuring compliance with legal requirements. We guide clients throughout the registration process, addressing any issues that may arise.

  • Trademark Infringement Litigation: We provide litigation services for trademark infringement cases.   

Copyright Protection

Copyright protects original works of authorship. Shree Legal offers:

  • Copyright Registration Services: We assist clients in registering their copyrights, ensuring that their creative works are legally protected.

  • Enforcement of Copyright Rights: If a client’s copyright is infringed, we provide representation in litigation to protect their rights and seek remedies for unauthorized use.

Patent Services

Patents protect inventions and innovations. Our firm provides:

  • Patent Application Preparation: We assist clients in preparing and filing patent applications, ensuring compliance with legal requirements and best practices for patentability.

  • Patent Enforcement: In cases of patent infringement, we advocate for our clients' rights through negotiation, mediation, and litigation.

Trade Secrets Protection

Trade secrets require special protection to maintain their value. Our services include:

  • Trade Secret Agreements: We help clients draft non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) and other contracts that protect sensitive information from unauthorized disclosure.

  • Litigation Support: In cases of trade secret misappropriation, our team provides legal representation to enforce trade secret rights and seek damages.
